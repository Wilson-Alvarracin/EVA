Los Colores del HTML

HEXADECIMAL COLORS

The most popular are Hex color codes; three byte hexadecimal numbers (that is, made up of six digits), with each byte, or a couple of characters in the Hex code, representing the intensity of red, green, and blue in the color, respectively.

TRANSNPARENT HEXADECIMAL COLORS

The base is the same but adding two figures to the total of 6 to interpret the transparency of the same

RGB

The meaning of RGB is the three primary colors, red, green, and blue. (Red, Green and Blue). All color on a screen can be represented as a mixture of these 3 colors, indicating the proportion of each with a number from 0 to 255.

RGBA

The system is exactly the same as RGB but now we add one more figure in the code to provide opacity or transparency to the color.

PRESET COLORS

The predefined color names are those that the css language can interpret just by inserting it verbatim.
